ABOUT US
Yangzhou Light Technology Development Co., Ltd. is specialized in new energy,permanent magnet motor,logistics sorting,linear drive motor research,development,production and sale of enterprises.The existing two series hub direct drive motors are used in new energy electric vehicles:At the same time,the mature wheel hub drive technology is applied to the production industrial ceiling fan;Self-developed linear motors are used in airports,fruits and vegetables,logistics,sorting,and modern flow-line equipment.We have more than 10 proprietary intellectual property rights core patent technology,and completely solve the demagnetization problem of permanent magnet motor.
